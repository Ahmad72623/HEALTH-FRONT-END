Health Risk Calculator

How to Use the Health Risk Calculator

Follow these steps to determine the health risk category:

  • Enter your age
  • Provide your weight in kilograms and height in centimeters
  • Enter your systolic and diastolic blood pressure readings
  • Select any family history of disease (optional)
  • Click "Calculate Risk" to get the result

Risk Levels:

  • Low Risk: Healthy condition
  • Moderate Risk: Some health concerns
  • High Risk: Increased risk factors present
  • Uninsurable: Serious health risks detected
